About Peoplesoft Connect

PeopleSoft Connect is the most comprehensive professional development conference and live showcase for PeopleSoft products and services. Each year, PeopleSoft invites its customers, suppliers, and partners to obtain the latest product information, meet PeopleSoft experts, attend training, mingle with thousands of PeopleSoft users, and hear the PeopleSoft strategic vision for the future.

And this vision has certainly evolved. We started by offering you “no code on the client” last year. Now we’ll take you one step further, showing you how to bring your enterprise to the next competitive level: true collaboration with your customers, suppliers, and employees.

We’re looking forward to an even more spectacular time at PeopleSoft Connect in 2001. We’ve chosen a location supporting stronger attendee interaction, honed our presentation and educational content based on last year’s feedback, and lined up a wealth of unbelievable evening events for you.
