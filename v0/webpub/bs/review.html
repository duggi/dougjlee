grind session

review by mega-flaizer >>>


ok, this game is seriously fun.  who cares if the controls are basically exactly the same as THPS - this game still rocks and has more than enough merits to stand on its own.  

one new feature is the skater's eye - you can look around the course and the lines will be made out for you in dot to dot fashion.  this is for all you lamers out there who can't hang with "finding the line".  well actually this is key for finding and grinding the tech lines - you'll discover that these so-called tech lines add serious playability to the game.  

in grind session you can build and save characters, earn respect, and skate every spot until you have mastered them all.  the tech lines are basically different combo runs and some of them are super hard to pull but definitely worth it as you get extra time for tech lines.  trying to plan your run to hit all the tech lines is a serious challenge and addicting as hell.

you have to build up respect to skate different spots by getting "pro points" and hitting sick combos.  that's pretty cool but i would like to see it called "mad props" instead.  also, what about the "pro-hoe points" yo.

you can also get "possessed to skate" if you don't fall and pull trick combos. basically you get powered up and can spin faster. i pulled an 1800 and a switch 540 christ air while possessed and it was hella cool, yo.

this game features daewon song and john cardiel.  well also ed templeton, cara-beth burnside, and pigpen.  there are 8 different courses and a house with skate rooms to unlock later, and of course there are secret characters. now i'm not supposed to talk about that but just to give you a clue think big brother.

there's a whole ton of tricks in this game for sick combos like 360 flip to nose grind backside 180 flip off to manual to 180.  well you get the idea.  the soundtrack is pretty live with some nice rap and hardcore mixed in there.  try NOFX, GZA, dr. octagon, and others.

well i would probably get this over THPS at the least because it's newer. well it looks like the skateboard video game wars are heating up and all fools are gonna come prepared so get ready to rumble!
