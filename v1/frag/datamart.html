Account Management Datamart

Prototype datamart/reporting system, used for aggregate reporting and rapid insight. DEMO COMING!


I was asked to revitalize an internal reporting tool that was on its final legs in a last ditch effort to save it from the brink.

The result was DataMart, a systems reporting aggregator that tied together disparate ERP, CRM, and SFA data into a single, unified view. It was wildly successful and is now the defacto account management tool -- there is even a training program behind it. One of my favorites!
